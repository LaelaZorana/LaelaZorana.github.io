About

An engineer who ships — and proves it.

My career has moved through systems where mistakes are expensive — banking, risk, and large operations. That taught me to prove things work before they ship, not after.

Now I point that discipline at AI. I build complete products: a grounded RAG assistant that won't answer without a citation, support automation that applies policy-correct refunds, an LLM red-team harness mapped to NIST and ISO governance, a smart-contract auditor, a high-throughput inference service, and a tested data pipeline with quality gates. Each one ships with the model and the evidence it works.

I'm comfortable across the whole stack — training and fine-tuning models, wiring them into clean FastAPI services and front-ends, and deploying with Docker, CI, and monitoring. I care about the unglamorous parts: tests, latency budgets, failure cases, and a demo a recruiter can run in under a minute.
