Background

About Me

I bring 10+ years of structured content QA and rubric-based evaluation from regulated environments — the kind of work where precision isn't optional. My career has centered on building and applying evaluation frameworks that hold up under scrutiny, whether auditing risk documentation at Deutsche Bank's Chief Risk Office or designing inter-rater reliability systems for complex content.

Now I'm channeling that discipline into AI training data quality and MLOps engineering. I build the tooling that sits at the quality layer of AI pipelines — the systems that catch bad data before it shapes model behavior. That means RLHF evaluation frameworks, content policy reviewers, annotation quality validators, and the MLOps infrastructure to run it all reliably at scale.

I'm finishing my MS in Cloud Computing at Purdue, hold a Vanderbilt AI Agents certificate, and am Lean Six Sigma certified. I'm at the intersection of structured review discipline and ML engineering — and I think that combination is exactly what the AI quality layer needs right now.
